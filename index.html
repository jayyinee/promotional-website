The Main Characters


May the Engineer

As an engineer, May is a sharp-thinking perfectionist. She is also the breadwinner of the family. While planning to divorce Cody, she suddenly turns into a doll and is now trying to break the spell to get back to her real body. Although it was a frustrating journey at first, May rediscover her childish side and love for singing later on.


Cody the Stay-at-Home Dad

Cody is caring stay-at-home dad, who has an interest in gardening. But he struggled with his nursery garden, and the business went bankrupt. He views himself as a failure because he couldn't provide for his family. But this is all because he is quick to give up and then blames others for his shortcomings.


Dr. Hakim the Nosy Self-proclaim Love Guru

Dr. Hakim claims to be the self-help love book titled "Book of Love." As an expert on love, he promises to Rose that he will fix her parents' marriage. Even though he might seems annoying at first due to his weird and dangerous methods, he is doing them for a good reason.


Rose the Sad Daughter

Rose, the daughter of May and Cody, grew up to be a happy girl with parents who love and care for her a lot. But after learning that her parents will soon drift apart, she makes wishes to the "Book of Love" and cries onto the scrap dolls she made of her parents, which magically turns her parents into the dolls!
